# CareerNest - AI Study Notes Generator User Guide

## Overview

The Study Notes Generator creates structured, exam-oriented study notes specifically designed for Maharashtra students following Maharashtra State Board and university syllabi.

## How to Use

### Step 1: Fill in the Form

1. **Education Level*** - Select your class/course level:
   - Class 1-5, 6-8, 9-10, or 11-12 (for school students)
   - Diploma, Engineering, BSc, BCA, BA, BCom (for college students)

2. **Board/University*** - Select your educational institution:
   - Maharashtra State Board (for school students)
   - Savitribai Phule Pune University
   - University of Mumbai
   - And 7 other Maharashtra universities

3. **Class/Semester*** - Enter your current class or semester
   - Example: "Class 10" or "Semester 3"

4. **Subject*** - Enter the subject name
   - Example: "Mathematics", "Physics", "History"

5. **Chapter/Topic*** - Enter the chapter or topic name
   - Example: "Quadratic Equations", "Newton's Laws"

6. **Difficulty Level*** - Choose content complexity:
   - **Easy:** Basic concepts, simple examples, foundational learning
   - **Medium:** Standard problems, typical exam questions
   - **Advanced:** Complex problems, critical thinking, advanced applications

7. **Additional Instructions** (Optional) - Add specific requirements or focus areas

### Step 2: Generate Notes

Click the **"Generate Study Notes"** button. The system will create comprehensive, structured notes following Maharashtra syllabus patterns.

### Step 3: View Your Notes

Your generated notes appear in the "Your Study Notes" section with:

1. **Cover Page Display**
   - CareerNest logo
   - Maharashtra Education branding
   - Board/University name
   - Subject and Chapter details

2. **Structured Content** (Click to expand sections)
   - Introduction
   - Key Concepts
   - Detailed Explanation
   - Important Points/Formulas
   - Solved Examples
   - Summary
   - Practice Questions

## Available Actions

### 📥 Download as PDF
- Click the download icon to save notes as PDF
- **Page 1:** CareerNest logo only (cover page)
- **Page 2+:** Complete study notes content
- Perfect for printing or offline study

### 📋 Copy to Clipboard
- Click the copy icon to copy full note content
- Paste into other applications (Word, Google Docs, etc.)
- Checkmark appears on success

### 🗑️ Delete Note
- Click the trash icon to permanently remove a note
- Cannot be undone

## PDF Download Tips

1. **Print Settings:**
   - Use "Save as PDF" option
   - Portrait orientation
   - A4 paper size
   - Color or black & white (both work)

2. **Browser Compatibility:**
   - Works best in Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari
   - Allow pop-ups if prompted
   - Download may take 2-3 seconds

3. **Quality:**
   - Logo appears centered on first page only
   - Content is professionally formatted
   - Readable fonts and spacing

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Submit Form
- Ensure all fields marked with * are filled
- Difficulty Level is now required

### Logo Not Showing
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Logo missing doesn't affect content quality

### PDF Download Not Working
- Allow pop-ups in browser settings
- Try a different browser
- Use "Copy" button as alternative

### Content Too Simple/Complex
- Adjust Difficulty Level
- Try different education level
- Add specific instructions
